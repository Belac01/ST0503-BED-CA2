How to get started

Start by registering your own account at the top right corner of the screen. After registering, you will be auto-logged in and can start interacting with the various features of the website. Once you have finished a task, create a new task progress in the task progress tab and rewards will be provided.

What each tab does

Tabs Purpose
Home A basic homepage containing basic information on what this app does.
Users Showcases all users currently registered with the website.
Tasks A list of all tasks that can be completed for points and Eco-coins
TaskProgress A list of tasks that you have completed. This is also where you come to complete tasks.
Shop A place where you can use your eco-coins to purchase cool items.
Inventory A place where you see the items you own.
Guilds Create a community or join one.
Messaging Forum A place for discussions or sharing of tips and tricks.
